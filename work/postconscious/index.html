Postconscious

Concept, Design & Development

Postconscious is a web experiment & art project that pulls the latest posts from Twitter and reads them aloud, with a minimal visual interface. A search input gives you the opportunity to hear current chatter on any nearly subject you can dream of. Read more about the project on The Verge.
